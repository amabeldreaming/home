About Me

I'm Amabel — a 40 year old Danish dreamer living at the intersection of romance, fantasy, technology, and storytelling.

I've been a reader my whole life. My mom took me to the library when I was very young and opened up a world I never really left. From The Secret Garden to Anne of Green Gables to romantasy and dark romance — stories have always been my safest place, my favorite way to travel.

I'm also a gamer, a K-drama obsessive, a K-pop rabbit hole survivor, and someone who thinks way too much about human connection and why we love the stories we love.

When AI image generation arrived I felt something click. I've always had so many images and feelings stuck in my head with no way to get them out — suddenly I had a way to tunnel them into the world and share them. I've been obsessed ever since.

This space is what happens when all of those things collide. Tiny experiences, small worlds, dark romance energy — made for women who love what I love.
